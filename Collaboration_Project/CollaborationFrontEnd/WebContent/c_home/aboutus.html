About Us


Mission

Founded in 2017, MingleBox's mission is to give people the power to share and make the world more open and connected. People use MingleBox to stay connected with friends, alumini,employees, to discover what's going on in the world, to share and express what matters to them through the blog and to share the new job opportunities available.
